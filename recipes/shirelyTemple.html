Shirely Temple

Description

A sweet and classy drink, the shirely temple is a classic. Served during any time of year at any occasion the shirely temple is a versatile beverage. With its extremely easy to make recipe the shirely temple is for anyone and everyone!

Ingredients

  • Sprite or ginger ale
  • Cherry grenadine syrup
  • Cherries (optional)

Steps

  • In a glass combine sprite or ginger ale with the cherry syrup
  • Mix and garnish with a cherry and you're done!
