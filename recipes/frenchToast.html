French Toast

Description

A classic breakfast dish that may or may not have originated in France. A light dusting of powdered sugar and drizzle of maple syrup ties this dish homely dish together. A great dish for all families.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of half and half
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teasponn of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 12 slices whole-wheat bread, or any bread of your choice
  • Powder Sugar
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  • Place a skillet over medium heat
  • Combine eggs and half and half into a large but shallow bowl. Beat to combine and stir in vanilla extract and salt.
  • Cover the bottom of the skillet with butter
  • Work in batches, soak the bread in the egg mixture for 1 minute on each side.
  • Lift bread out of mixture and let it drain a little. Then place the bread on the skillet and let it cook for about 5-8 minutes or until golden brown, flip once and allow this side to cook. Repeat with other slices.
  • Serve immediately with toppings
